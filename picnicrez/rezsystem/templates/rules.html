RULES AND REGULATIONS

REFUND POLICY:
  • Refund or rescheduling requests must be received at least 14 days prior to event date.
  • Refunds are not given due to inclement weather and rain dates will not be issued.
  • Payments originally made by cash or check will be returned by check from the City of Newport News and can take 3-4 weeks to process.
  • Payments originally made by credit card will be issued to the original credit card used and may take 7-10 business days to process.

RULES:
  • By permit only: amusement rides/equipment, pony rides, petting zoo, dunk tanks, weddings, stage use, tents, generators, amplified sound, fundraising, vending, etc.
  • Requests for special permits must be submitted via a Special Activity Application at least 3 WEEKS in advance. Call (757) 886-7912 for information.
  • All city parks are open sunrise to sunset.
  • Park staff guarantees that your reserved shelter will be cleaned by 10 am.
  • Reserved shelters will be posted with group name on the day of the picnic.
  • Please park in designated parking spaces only.
  • Picnic supplies must be hand carried from the parking lot to the shelter. Motor vehicles cannot be driven to the picnic shelter.
  • The pavilion should be left in good condition; i.e. all trash placed in trash cans and all excess trash should be taken offsite or to the closest dumpster. Please pick up all litter from the ground.
  • Any violation of rules or capacity limits may result in termination of your event.
  • If you would like assistance with planning a reunion, please call (757) 886-7777.
PROHIBITED ITEMS:
  • Outside grills and fryers.
  • Balloons, balloon releases, water balloons, pinatas, confetti/glitter, silly string, sidewalk chalk, gender reveal items that explode or expel items.
  • Use of staples, nails, tape or tacks for hanging items on picnic shelters.
  • Alcoholic beverages.
  • Amplified music and/or sound.
  • Onsite collection of money, sale of foods, services or other products
  • Dogs off leash.
Any violations of rules or capacity limits may result in termination of your event.
ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS

Call (757) 888-3333 for staff assistance or for park ranger.
