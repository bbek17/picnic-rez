Picnic Shelters

PICNIC IN
OUR PARKS

Newport News has 33 picnic shelters in eight parks across the City that can be reserved.
The city offers three sizes of shelters that accommodate different levels of people:
  • A Full Shelter, which accommodates approximately 35 people, has three to four tables and one grill.
  • A Large Shelter, which accomodates approximately 75 people, has eight tables and one large grill.
  • A Group Shelter, which accommodates approximately 100 people, has fifteen tables and one large grill.

Electricity is available at some shelters. All parks are open Sunrise to Sunset. Reserved shelters are cleaned by 10:00 a.m. Picnics with special activities (rental equipment, entertainment, weddings, etc.) must contact the Special Activity Coordinator (757-886-7912) for permit information.

Reservations can be charged over the phone by calling 757-888-3333 or toll free at 1-800-203-8322.
Reservations can also be made in person at the Newport News Park Campsite Office, Deer Park, or the Parks & Recreation Office at 700 Town Center Drive (3rd floor). Credit cards are accepted at the Newport News Park Campsite Office.
