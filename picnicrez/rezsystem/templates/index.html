Newport News has 35 picnic shelters in 8 parks across the City that can be reserved for picnic activities.
The city offers three sizes of shelters that accommodate different levels of people:

  • Shelter Size
    # of People

  • Group

    $200/day

General Shelter Information:
  • Payment for the shelter is due at time of reservation.

  • Shelter Reservations are good for the entire day

  • Electricity is available, at some shelters, for an additional $10.

  • All parks are open Sunrise to Sunset.

  • Reserved shelters are guaranteed to be cleaned by 10:00 a.m.

  • Picnics with special activities (rental equipment, entertainment, weddings, etc.) must contact the Special Activity Coordinator (757-886-7912) for permit information.

Reservations can be made over the phone by calling 757-888-3333 or toll free at 1-800-203-8322.
Reservations can also be made in person at the Newport News Park Campsite Office, Deer Park, or the Parks & Recreation Office at 700 Town Center Drive (3rd floor).
Credit cards are accepted at the Newport News Park Campsite Office.
