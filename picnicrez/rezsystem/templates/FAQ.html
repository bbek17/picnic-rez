Frequently Asked Questions

The shelters are guranteed to be clean by 10 am, but you are welcome to set up as early as you would like to. The shelter reservation is good until sunset on the day of your rental.

Our cancellation policy allows returns more than 14 days of reservation date. Calls within the 14 period would allow for a rescheduling of the picnic shelter reservation.

The amount of time it takes to receive the issued refunds depends on the method of payment. Credit/Debit payments can be expected in 5 to 10 business days. Cash payments have to be returned as a check and can take several weeks to be processed through City Center.

Depending the size of the shelter the amount of tables varies:

  • Our Full shelter, which holds up to 35 people, typically has 3 to 4 tables
  • Our Large shelter, which holds up to 70 people, typically has 8 tables
  • Our Group shelter, which holds up to 100 people, typically has 15 tables

Each shelter comes with a charcoal grill that is sized to accommodate cooking for the maximum number of guests that the shelter can handle.

The city provides one large charcoal at each shelter. Outside grills are prohibited unless it is around the size of a laptop, ie a single burner or a George Forman. Check our rules page for more information on what is prohibited. An example for what may be allowed:
